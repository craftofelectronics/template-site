For a bridge circuit to be "balanced" means that there is zero voltage between the two opposite corners of the circuit (where the battery does not) connect. Achieving a condition of "balance" in a bridge circuit requires that the resistance ratios of the four "arms" of the circuit be in proportion:


${R_1 \over R_3} = {R_2 \over R_4}$
