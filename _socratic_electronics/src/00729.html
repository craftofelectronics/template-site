Range = 500 mA

Determining the ranges for this ammeter is simply an exercise in Ohm’s Law. It is very important that your students recognize the shunt resistor’s value as being in milliohms and not Megaohms! Yes, there is a difference between a lower-case letter "m" and a capital letter "M"!
