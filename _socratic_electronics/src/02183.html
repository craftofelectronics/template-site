Follow-up question: is this the only way we could have used the oscilloscope to measure phase shift between voltage and current, or is there another mode of operation besides plotting Lissajous figures?

Ask your students to explain the function of the resistor Rshunt shown in the schematic diagram. Discuss whether or not this resistor should have a very low or a very high resistance value. Also discuss the placement of the oscilloscope’s ground clip, which is very important in a potentially lethal AC power circuit.
