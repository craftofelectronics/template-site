Really, there is only one logical place to check after seeing that TP10 is high, and that is the input of the XOR gate (TP2 and TP6), because we need to know whether the XOR gate is being told to go high (i.e. TP2 and TP6 in different states) or whether the XOR gate output is stuck high.
