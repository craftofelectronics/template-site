Explain the functional difference between an overload heater and a fuse or circuit breaker in a motor control circuit. Fuses and circuit breakers perform one type of overcurrent protection, while overload heaters perform quite another!

Fuses and circuit breakers protect the wiring carrying power to a load. Overload heaters protect the load itself from thermal damage induced by excessive current.
