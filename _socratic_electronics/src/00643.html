Follow-up question: does the addition of the capacitor affect the amount of current through the 5 Ω  load? Why or why not?

The answers to this question may seem really strange to students accustomed to DC circuit calculations, where parallel branch currents always add up to a greater total. With complex numbers, however, the sum is not necessarily greater than the individual values!
