Really, there is only one logical place to check after seeing that TP13 is low and we have power (TP14 is high) and that is the input of the NOR gate (TP9 though TP12), because we need to know whether the NOR gate is being told to go low (i.e. a high input) or whether the NOR gate output is stuck low. Nothing to the right of the NOR gate matters because we know the NOR gate isn’t even telling the SSR to energize (TP13 is low).
