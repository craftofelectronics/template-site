Answer the following electrical safety questions:

 •  What can electric current do to your motor muscles (fingers, arms, legs) if enough of it passes through your body?

 •  What can electric current do to your heart and lung muscles if enough of it passes through your body?

 •  What special kind of hand tools are best for working on live power circuits?

 •  What type of specific medical attention may be needed for a victim of electric shock?

 •  What can electric current do to your motor muscles (fingers, arms, legs) if enough of it passes through your body? It may cause those muscles to involuntarily contract, placing you in danger of sustained electric shock.

 •  What can electric current do to your heart and lung muscles if enough of it passes through your body? It may paralyze those muscles, resulting in death.

 •  What special kind of hand tools are best for working on live power circuits? Tools with electrically-insulated handles.

 •  What type of specific medical attention may be needed for a victim of electric shock? CPR, to revive a stopped or fibrillating heart.
