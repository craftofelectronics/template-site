Also, write the equation describing the operating frequency of this type of oscillator circuit.

This is an Armstrong oscillator circuit, and the combination of capacitor C3 and primary transformer winding inductance L1 establishes its frequency of operation.


$f = {1 \over {2 \pi \sqrt{L_1 C_3}}}$
