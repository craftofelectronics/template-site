Answer the following electrical safety questions:

 •  What does electric current do to human muscle tissue?

 •  Why should you work with just one hand on live power circuits instead of with both hands?

 •  Which hand should you work with, if just one?

 •  What type of specific medical attention may be needed for a victim of electric shock?

 •  What does electric current do to human muscle tissue? Causes it to contract.

 •  Why should you work with just one hand on live power circuits? To minimize chance of arm-to-arm shock current path.

 •  Which hand should you work with, if just one? The right hand.

 •  What type of specific medical attention may be needed for a victim of electric shock? CPR, to revive a stopped or fibrillating heart.
