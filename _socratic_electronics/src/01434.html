It is common to see a capacitor connected between the "Control" terminal and ground in 555 timer circuits, especially when precise timing is important.


$\epsfbox{01434x01.eps}$

Explain what purpose the capacitor C2 serves in this circuit.

C2 acts as a decoupling capacitor, to help stabilize the threshold and trigger reference voltages internal to the 555.

Challenge question: what operational parameters of the circuit define the necessary capacitance value of C2?

Decoupling power supply pins on a chip is important, but here students get to see another variation of decoupling.
