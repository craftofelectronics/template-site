Generally speaking, is an ammeter supposed to be connected in series or in parallel with the component it’s measuring current through?

Series.

If your students don’t already know, this question is an excellent opportunity for them to learn about what a "series" circuit is and how it differs from a "parallel" circuit. Ask your students why "series" is the correct answer. What is it about a series connection that makes it the best type of connection to use an ammeter in? What would happen if we connected an ammeter in parallel with a circuit component?
