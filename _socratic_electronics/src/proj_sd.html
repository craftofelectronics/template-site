Your project is to build a "sensitive audio detector:" a device useful for detecting low-level electrical signals and expressing them in the form of sound. Here is a sample schematic diagram for you to follow:


$\epsfbox{proj_sd1.eps}$

I recommend you follow this design closely, to ensure your detector will function in a safe manner. The headphones should be of the "passive" type, meaning no built-in amplifier or sound controls – just a plain set of 8 Ω  closed-cup headphones. If you wish to make any design changes, please consult your instructor.
