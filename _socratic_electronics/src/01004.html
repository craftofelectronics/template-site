Write a mathematical expression in the form of y = …x describing the function of this circuit:


$\epsfbox{01004x01.eps}$

y =  − x

Your students should be able to recognize that this amplifier circuit has a voltage gain of 1, and that it is inverting in nature, but expressing it in the form of an equation using variables such as x and y may be something very new to them. Discuss with your students the significance of this notation: that a circuit may embody an equation.
