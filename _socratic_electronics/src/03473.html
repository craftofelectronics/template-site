Suppose a magnet is moved toward a coil of wire, which is in turn connected to a variable resistance (potentiometer connected as a rheostat):

Determine the following:

 •  The polarity of the induced voltage (+ and -)

 •  The direction of current through the resistance (conventional flow notation assumed)

 •  The direction of physical force exerted on the coil as a result of Lenz’s Law.

 •  Whether the resistance needs to increase or decrease to maximize this force on the coil; or whether the amount of resistance is irrelevant to force.
