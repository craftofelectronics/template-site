How does the total resistance (RT) of two parallel-connected resistors relate to the individual resistor values (R1 and R2)? Express your answer in the form of an equation.


$\epsfbox{03402x01.eps}$


$R_T = {{R_1 R_2} \over {R_1 + R_2}}$

. . . or . . .


$R_T = {1 \over {{1 \over R_1} + {1 \over R_2}}}$
