Use circuit simulation software to verify your predicted and measured parameter values.

I’ve used 47 μF electrolytic capacitors and 1N4001 diodes with good success on a 10 volt AC (RMS) power supply. I recommend that students measure their own diodes to determine typical forward voltage (VF).

Don’t forget to mention the polarity sensitivity of these capacitors! Electrolytic capacitors can explode violently if reverse-connected!
