Ask your students to explain the mathematics behind this answer. What procedure gives them the quantity of 33 Ω  from the given component values? Why is this answer only approximate? What factors might affect it?

Also, ask your students to explain why the common-collector transistor stage does not require a biasing network or coupling capacitor, as the common-emitter stage does.
