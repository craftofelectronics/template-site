What is the difference between a plug and a jack? What are these two devices used for? Draw the schematic diagram symbols for single-conductor plugs and jacks.

A "plug" is the male half of a temporary electrical connector, while the "jack" is the female half. Electrical connectors are used to make convenient connections between individual wires or sets of wires.


$\epsfbox{00232x01.eps}$

Be sure to inform your students that poor electrical connections, often with regard to plug-and-jack assemblies, are a very common source of failure in electrical and electronic systems.
