ELTR 145 (Digital 2), section 3

Students often mix up the identities of encoders, decoders, multiplexers, and demultiplexers. It is important for them to realize that while multiplexers and demultiplexers contain decoders, they are not the same as decoders (or encoders). In essence, muxes and demuxes "steer" signals to/from different locations, while encoders and decoders convert data from one format to another.
