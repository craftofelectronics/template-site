How is the parameter of zener impedance defined for a zener diode? Should an ideal zener diode have a zener impedance figure equal to zero, or infinite? Why?

$Z_{zener} = {{\Delta E_{diode}} \over {\Delta I_{diode}}}$ or $Z_{zener} = {{d E_{diode}} \over {d I_{diode}}}$

(The "d" is a calculus symbol, representing a change of infinitesimal magnitude.)

Ideally, a zener diode will have a zener impedance of zero ohms.
