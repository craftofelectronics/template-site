A unijunction transistor with an intrinsic standoff ratio (η) of 0.8 is powered by a 15 volt DC source. Calculate the emitter voltage needed to "trigger" this UJT into its conductive state.


$\epsfbox{02140x01.eps}$

VP ≈  12.7 volts

Nothing special here, just practice calculating the trigger voltage. Note to your students that the symbol for the intrinsic standoff ratio (η) is the Greek letter "eta," which also happens to be used to symbolize efficiency.
