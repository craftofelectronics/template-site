This worksheet covers the following concepts:

 •  Basic physics terms: energy, work, and power.

 •  Precise definitions of voltage and current.

 •  Using Ohm’s Law to calculate voltage, current, and resistance.

 •  Plotting measured and calculated data on graphs.

 •  Relating electrical resistance to graphical plots.

 •  Using Ohm’s/Joule’s Laws to calculate power.

 •  Algebraic manipulation of Ohm’s Law equations.

 •  Simple series and simple parallel resistor circuits.

 •  Short circuits.
