What diode performance parameter establishes the limit for maximum frequency of AC which it may rectify? If you were to examine a diode datasheet, what parameter (or parameters) would be the most important in answering this question?

Reverse Recovery Time (trr) is a very important parameter limiting maximum rectification frequency. Junction capacitance (Cj) is another.

Ask your students to describe what the "ideal" trr and Cj values would be for a diode with unlimited rectification bandwidth.
